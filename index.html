Cell, Molecular, and Developmental Biology

Amongst the academic subjects that I have taken, the one that inspired me the most is Biology. I have always been fascinated with the complexity of life and living organisms and the effects of its interactions. My interest in Biology has encouraged me and has led me to excel in high-level Biology courses such as Honors Biology and Advanced Placement Biology. These classes not only furthered my understanding but also cultivated my passion for the subject. It has inspired me to extend my learning outside the rudimentary classroom environment into an intellectually challenging extracurricular activity such as Academic decathlon. My passion on topics involving Biology intensified through my involvement in Academic Decathlon; I have specifically been intellectually stimulated by the topics on cell cycle, the effects of disturbance in the cycle that may lead to cancer and its progression. Having excelled in the county and state competition garnering 4th and 3rd place in Science is a testament to my interest in this field. I have always desired to give back and make a contribution to the community. I consider myself fortunate that I was given a chance to volunteer at St Jude Medical Center. I chose to work at the Oncology department so I could dedicate my services to staff that supported my cousin Chloe’s battle with cancer. Through such experience, I have realized various important lessons which I believe will help me through life and gain insight into the profession that I aspire for. Not only was I able to gain experience of being in a hospital environment, but the opportunity to be able to observe, understand further and apply some knowledge I gained from the Biology classes and academic decathlon in a real world-scenario like patients undergoing chemotherapy and how it affects the treatment or progression of cancer over time. I have an ardent desire to pursue this subject at a UC by majoring in a medical-oriented major that will provide me with a great foundation in achieving my ultimate dream of becoming a pediatrician whom I envision would help make a difference in other people’s lives.
